Hassle-Free, Instant Results

We know that meters aren't pretty.
They're messy, confusing, and impossible to understand.

That's why we've created openMTR.

Free to Use, Easy to Understand

openMTR allows you to upload a picture of your meter and instantly recieve the information you're looking for.
We've used image recogition technology to build a smarter meter-reader just for you.

Open Source

It's important to us that technology is available and free to everyone, so we've created an open source API that you can incorporate into your project or business.

Check out our Documentation Page for more information on integration.
