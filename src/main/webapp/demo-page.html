Why do you need my email?
Your Privacy is Important to Us

Here at OpenMTR, we completely understand people can be hesitant about providing information online. The reason we're asking is for documentation and data gathering only. In order to improve our application, we keep metrics about the number of users, successes, processing time, etc. And email is one of the ways we track that.

We promise never to sell your information, send you spam, or do anything we might find annoying, should we be in your shoes.

Thank you so much for your participation. We want to make this the highest quality we can - so we can give you the most accurate results!
